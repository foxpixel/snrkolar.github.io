History


Kolar is a District in the state Karnataka located with a Latitude 13.17 N longitude of 78.20 E, Being District Head Quarters Kolar consists of 5 Talukas Bangarpet ,KGF , Malur , Mulbagal ,Srinivaspura. Kolar District being Drought Land is Famous for growing Vegetables, Fruits especially Mangoes from Srinivaspura, Milk and Silk City of Karnataka.

In the Heart of Kolar District Lies the Most Famous and Oldest Hospital of Kolar District, Sri Narashima Raja District Hospital, which was built by Bhadoor Sri Krishna raja wadiyar in the year 1937 in an area of 16 Acres with a capacity of 100 Beds. The Hospital was Built in complete Stones in those Days and still a Engineering Marvel till date with good Ventilation.

Presently Kolar District Has a population of 18Lakhs and SNR District Hospital is the Major Territory care unit with 600beds capacity is Serving all cadre patients equally with good and quality Health care services around the clock.

Vission and Mission


To be distinguished place of study and practice that nurtures a diverse and an inclusive community that is dedicated to mitigating pain and suffering and enhancing the health and well being by striving for excellence in teaching and learning, patient care and leadership.

SNR Strives to create a new era of doctors who are trained to the highest levels of ability and accomplishment, guided mightily by proffesional ethics, ready to serve and lead the world by example. It will vigourously foster and protext the values and ethics of health care and will serve all without prejudice of any kind.
