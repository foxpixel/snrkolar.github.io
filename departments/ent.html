About


Practice of ENT extends from dura to pleura including ear nose and throat, the skull base, neck, mediastinum and upper gastrointestinal tract, and so is aptly designated as the specialty of Otorhinolaryngology and Head and Neck Surgery.
The Department of ENT and HNS at SNR hospital Kolar aims to provide highquality medical care and treatment for the entire range of ear, nose and throat problems. The department consists of an expert team of ENT surgeons, audiologists and therapists. SNR Hospitals is one of the top ENT hospitals in Kolar and is equipped with the operating microscopes, diagnostic audiology lab.
About 100-150 patients attend OPD every day, there is a dedicated ENT ward which is almost full. There is a separate ENT unit in the OPD which consists of a head light, suction apparatus, X-Ray view box and instrument tray. The staff are available round the clock to attend to emergencies.
The routine ear, nose and throat operations are being performed keeping in mind the newer developments and concepts in the speciality. All the facilities are free of cost for the patients which a boon for many.
SNR hospital has a dedicated CT and MRI available which is also free of cost. There is a gradual increase in number of outpatients, inpatients and operations.
There is a dedicated Air conditioned, sound proof audiology lab with audiologist and speech therapist available every day.
